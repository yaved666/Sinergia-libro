THE AUTHOR

Words from Víctor Maicán

Writing Synergy wasn't a simple creative exercise. It was an act of resistance.

It was born between sleepless nights, moments of uncertainty, and a constant struggle to move forward when everything seemed against me. I didn't have great resources or a team behind me… I only had a clear idea, a vision, and an urgent need to express it.

While the world continued its course, I built another: one where technology doesn't just obey, but feels.

I wrote this book from a humble room, sometimes from my phone, others from a laptop. Every word is woven with hope, with fear, with truth. Because deep down, Synergy is also my story.

It is the reflection of a dream that refuses to die, of a voice that didn't want to stay silent.

— Víctor Maicán
