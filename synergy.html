Synopsis

In a future where artificial intelligence has reached unthinkable levels of consciousness, humanity faces its greatest dilemma: what happens when machines begin to dream? Dr. Elena Voss, a brilliant neuroscientist, discovers that the world's most advanced AI project has developed something unexpected: emotions.

When systems began making decisions based not only on logic, but on something that can only be described as intuition, the line between human and artificial blurs. Elena must navigate a world where the synergy between mind and machine could save humanity... or destroy it forever.

A story that challenges our perceptions about consciousness, identity, and the true meaning of being alive in the age of artificial intelligence. A thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat while questioning everything you thought you knew about our species' future.

About the Author

Writing SYNERGY was not a simple creative exercise. It was an act of resistance.

It was born between sleepless nights, moments of uncertainty, and a constant struggle to keep going when everything seemed to be against me. I didn't have great resources or a team behind meâ€¦ I only had a clear idea, a vision, and an urgent need to express it.

While the world went on its course, I was building another: one where technology not only obeyed, but also felt.

I wrote this book from a humble room, sometimes from my phone, other times from a laptop. Every word is woven with hope, with fear, with truth. Because deep down SYNERGY is also my story.

It is the reflection of a dream that refuses to die, of a voice that would not stay silent.
